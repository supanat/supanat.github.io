Oral Animation Simulation

Oral Animation Simulation, based on a question, what happened inside the oral cavity when humans speak? We want to show a movement each part from the windpipe, tongue, teeth, through lips. To achieve an objective, I have to develop a 3D application from scratch for rendering an oral cavity in x-ray mode, animation blending capabilities to simulate animation of any English word.
